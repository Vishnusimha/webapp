How do I order?

Check on our instagram page for more themes, decide what you want to order and place an order through phone/instagram/Facebook

Can I pick up my order?

Yes, You can pick up your order from our address

Can I order for delivery as a gift?

Yes, we love delivering some smiles to your love one. NOTE that somebody must be present to accept the delivery & Delivery charges apply

How soon should I place my order?

Minimum a month for large items & minimum 3 days for gift bouquets

I would like to order a Gender Reveal Set up or balloon bouquet, but I don't want to know the gender of the baby...

You need not worry; your mother, sister, closest friend, or your doctor's office can email us to communicate the gender test findings. In order to book your day, we suggest that you place your order one month in advance. However, we accept gender results up to 24 hours before your event.

How long do they last?

The lifespan of your balloon cannot be guaranteed by us; it simply depends on how much direct sunlight, heat, cold, wind, etc. it experiences. Nevertheless, we make every effort to prolong the lives of our balloons as much as is humanly feasible. However, we cannot be held liable for how the weather may impact the setup or if any balloons pop once they have been delivered to your event safely.

After my event, what happens to the balloons?

You own the balloons because you paid for them. You have two choices when our staff returns to break down your setup: either they keep all of your balloons or they have to be popped by our team.
