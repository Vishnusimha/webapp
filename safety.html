SAFETY INSTRUCTIONS
  1. Helium is not harmful, but intentionally inhaling it can cause suffocation.
  2. Keep all kids away from helium cylinders and balloons.
  3. Store the helium cylinder in an upright position and secure properly to prevent toppling.
  4. Always keep the helium cylinder in a well ventilated area.Do not store in damp areas.Fire hazards and sources of heat should be avoided.
