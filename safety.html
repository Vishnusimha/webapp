INSTRUCTIONS
  1. Store the cylinder in an upright position and secure properly to prevent toppling.
  2. Keep all children away from cylinders and balloon filling apparatus.
  3. Always keep the cylinder in a well ventilated area.Do not store in damp areas.Fire hazards and sources of heat should be avoided.
  4. In the event of a fire,vacate the area and call the Fire Brigade informing them that the cylinder of balloon gas is there.
  5. In the event of a fire,vacate the area and call the Fire Brigade informing them that the cylinder of balloon gas is there.
  6. To avoid accidents,as balloon gas is stored at high pressure,the cylinder valve should never be opened without the appropriate filling kit attached and should be tightly closed when the cylinder is not in use.
  7. To avoid damage and potential accidents do not move cylinder with filling unit attached.
  8. Do not deliberately inhale helium – although not poisonous , it can result in suffocation.
  9. In the event of a fire,vacate the area and call the Fire Brigade informing them that the cylinder of balloon gas is there.
